Download Twig from http://twig.sensiolabs.org/ and move it to sites/all/libraries
such that it is located at sites/all/libraries/Twig (with a file located
at sites/all/libraries/Twig/lib/Twig/Autoloader.php).

(Optional) To enable syntax highlighting, download CodeMirror 3.01 or later from
http://codemirror.net/ and move it to sites/all/libraries such that it is located
at sites/all/libraries/codemirror (with a file located at
sites/all/libraries/codemirror/lib/codemirror.js).
